Questions?

Ready for Counselbay? Here are a few more things you might want to know.

How long are the contracts?

We don't intend to tie you down. Choose to go with our monthly plan and have the freedom to move on anytime! However, if you choose to go with our annual plan, we will gladly give you a two month discount.

What are Content Concierge Credit?

Our packages come with 10 credits each. Each credit can be used for to get our support team to perform a 5 min tweak to your website. Complex tweaks might need more than one credit. Turnaround time is within 2 business days, but usually faster.

What support options do i have?

We usually support our clients via our support ticketing system. Non-critical support is available between SGT 0930 - 1800. If you need an urgent critical situation that needs to be resolved, we are available 24/7.

Is there a setup fee?

Yes. There is a setup fee. When you sign up, we get cracking with the hardest part of hosting any website: the setup. Within 24 hours, we start taking care of everything that happens on the server level, from database to DNS to user creations. Then we work with you to configure your domain name.

What types of payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit card payments (Mastercard, Visa, American Express and Discover) via PayPal. For annual payments, we can invoice you for payment by cheque, on request.

Can i cancel my account at any time?

With Counselbay there are no contracts, and we never lock people in. All plans on Counselbay are either month-to-month or annual, and can be canceled at any time.
